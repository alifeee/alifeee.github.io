Sheffield Council Open Data # from Sheffield Council Open Data.md

last edited: 2025-08-26 11:57:22 +0100

Sheffield Council publish open data using arcgis. The search page shows all the published data:

  • https://sheffield-city-council-open-data-sheffieldcc.hub.arcgis.com/search

There's a lot of map data. My favourite map data are:

  • CCTV and Traffic Cameras
  • Benches on the highway network
  • Contaminated Land (there is only one entry — an old landfill)
  • Grit bins
  • Public Toilets

…and an honourable mention goes to these maps:

  • Allotment Sites
  • Libraries
  • Air Quality Diffusion Tubes
  • Air Quality Real-Time Monitors
  • Sheffield Local Heritage List

Secondly, there's also data that isn't in map form, such as:

  • Street Lighting Information
  • Gas Destructor Lights !!!
  • Street Maintenance Policy 2025

A dishonourable mention goes to these maps which seem to have been made private (I can't access them):

  • Historic Parks and Gardens
  • Houses in Multiple Occupation Article 4
  • Listed Buildings
  • Ancient Monuments

There's lots more data on the search page linked above. Have a look!

(I think) this data is published in part due to the "Local Government Transparency Code" (seemingly most of the maps) and (from reading some of the documents) responses to Freedom of Information requests end up on there too.

love open data ;]
